Caramilk Cheesecake Recipe

Description

Caramilk cheesecakes are the best. Here is my world famous family recipe with lots of sugar

Be sure to follow this recipe to a T for the best results.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of your favourite biscuits
  • 500g Philadelhpia Caramilk flavoured cream cheese
  • 400g sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 teaspoons powdered gelatine
  • Cadbury Caramilk chocolate shards for decorating

Instructions

  1. COMBINE the biscuit crumbs and butter, then press into the base and 3/4 of the way up the sides of a buttered and lined 20cm round springform pan.
  2. BEAT the PHILY with the condensed milk and vanilla until smooth and combined. Stir in gelatine mixture.
  3. POUR mixture over the prepared base. Refrigerate for 3 hours or until set.
  4. DECORATE as desired and serve.
